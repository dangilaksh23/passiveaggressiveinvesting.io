02The Financial Picture

OGC's financial performance is nothing short of impressive. For the most recent fiscal year (2025), the company reported revenues of $1893.2 million, representing a remarkable 46.3% year-over-year growth. This builds on a strong foundation, with a revenue CAGR of 25.1%, indicating sustained and significant top-line expansion. These figures place OGC firmly in the upper echelons of its sector for growth momentum.

Profitability is equally stellar. OGC achieved a Free Cash Flow (FCF) Margin of 28.6%, placing it in the 93rd percentile relative to its sector peers. Its Operating Margin of 41.1% is also outstanding, ranking in the 90th percentile. These metrics highlight exceptional operational efficiency and pricing power. Furthermore, I observe improving trends in FCF, Cash Conversion Cycle (CCC Days), and Return on Equity (ROE), suggesting management is consistently enhancing shareholder value and operational performance. The company’s CCC Days are a negative -27.0, meaning it collects cash before paying suppliers, a sign of incredible working capital management.

The balance sheet is pristine, with a Net Debt/EBITDA ratio of -0.37x, indicating a net cash position. This provides immense financial flexibility and resilience, especially valuable in a cyclical industry like gold. While the trailing PE of 7.74 and Price/Book of 2.52 seem quite modest for a company with such high quality and growth, this could reflect the current market's assessment of the gold sector or the technical "topping" signal I'm observing.
