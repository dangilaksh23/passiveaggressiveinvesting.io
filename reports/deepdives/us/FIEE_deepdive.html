Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

FiEE, Inc. is transforming from a legacy networking hardware provider to a digital service provider focused on AI and data analytics for content creation, digital authentication, and brand management. The company operates a cloud-based SaaS platform that helps clients develop, manage, and optimize their digital presence, including content creation and brand strategy. FiEE also offers custom software development and digital authentication services for art collections, leveraging AI and blockchain technology. Its competitive distinction lies in its integrated approach to digital brand management and its focus on emerging technologies.
