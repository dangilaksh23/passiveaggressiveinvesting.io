Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

VeriSign is a global provider of critical internet infrastructure and domain name registry services, essential for internet navigation and e-commerce. The company operates the authoritative directory for major top-level domains like .com and .net, and also provides Root Zone Maintainer services, ensuring the security and stability of the Domain Name System (DNS). Its competitive distinction lies in its exclusive role as the registry for the .com and .net domains, which handle a significant portion of global e-commerce, and its operation of core internet root servers.
