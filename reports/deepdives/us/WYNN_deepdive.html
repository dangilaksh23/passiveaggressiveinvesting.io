Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

Wynn Resorts designs, develops, and operates integrated luxury resorts that combine high-end hotels, retail, dining, entertainment, and gaming. The company generates revenue primarily through its casino operations, hotel stays, retail leases, and food and beverage services. Wynn Resorts differentiates itself through its extensive design and operational experience, a focus on superior guest experiences, and a commitment to luxury service, aiming to attract premium guests both domestically and internationally.
