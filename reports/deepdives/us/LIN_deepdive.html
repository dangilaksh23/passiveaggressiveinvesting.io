Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Linde is the world's largest industrial gas company, producing and distributing atmospheric and process gases like oxygen, nitrogen, hydrogen, and helium. It also designs and builds gas production equipment and offers related services. The company serves a broad range of industries, including healthcare, chemicals, manufacturing, and electronics, leveraging its technological innovation and extensive distribution network to provide cost efficiencies and operational improvements for its customers.
