Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Waste Management, Inc. is North America's leading provider of comprehensive environmental solutions, offering services across the United States and Canada. The company manages waste from collection to disposal, focusing on resource recovery and the creation of clean, renewable energy. Following its acquisition of Stericycle, Waste Management also provides regulated waste and compliance services, and secure information destruction. Its competitive distinction lies in its extensive asset network, including the largest landfill network in North America, and a commitment to sustainability and technological innovation.
