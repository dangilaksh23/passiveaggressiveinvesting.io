Business Model · 10-K 2025-02

Capital Properties, Inc. is a real estate investment company that primarily generates revenue by leasing land in downtown Providence, Rhode Island, under long-term ground leases. The company owns approximately 18 acres in the Capital Center Area, which it leases to developers and for public parking. It also leases billboard locations through a subsidiary. Its competitive distinction lies in its ownership of strategically located land in a developed urban area, with leases structured to provide periodic rent increases and contingent rent participation.
