Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Planet Fitness is a leading franchisor and operator of fitness centers, distinguished by its "Judgement Free Zone" philosophy, offering a welcoming and non-intimidating environment for all fitness levels. The company generates revenue primarily through membership dues and royalties from its extensive franchisee network, which operates over 2,800 clubs globally. Its competitive edge lies in its strong brand recognition, a highly accessible value proposition starting at $15 per month, and a scalable, franchisee-centric growth model.
