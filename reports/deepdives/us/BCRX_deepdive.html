Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

BioCryst Pharmaceuticals is a global biotechnology company focused on developing and commercializing treatments for rare diseases, particularly hereditary angioedema (HAE). Their flagship product, ORLADEYO, is an oral therapy for HAE prevention, supported by an established commercial infrastructure. The company leverages internal discovery and strategic business development to build a pipeline of potential first-in-class or best-in-class therapeutics, aiming for sustainable growth by focusing on rare disease markets and efficient resource allocation.
