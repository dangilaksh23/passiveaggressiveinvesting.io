Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

United States Lime Minerals, Inc. is a manufacturer of lime and limestone products, serving diverse industries including construction, industrial, environmental, metals, and agriculture. The company extracts limestone from its own quarries and mines, then processes it into various higher-value products like quicklime and hydrated lime. Its competitive advantage lies in its established operations, diverse customer base, and ability to produce high-quality products, though it is sensitive to regional market dynamics and input cost fluctuations.
