Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Newmont Corporation is the world's leading gold company, primarily focused on gold production with significant operations across the Americas, Papua New Guinea, Australia, and Ghana. The company also produces copper, silver, lead, and zinc as co-products. Newmont aims to create value through sustainable and responsible mining, leveraging its extensive land position and proven and probable gold reserves. Its competitive distinction lies in its scale as the largest gold producer globally and its commitment to responsible mining practices.
