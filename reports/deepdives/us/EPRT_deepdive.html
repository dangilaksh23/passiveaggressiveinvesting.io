Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

EPR Properties is a real estate investment trust (REIT) that owns and manages single-tenant properties primarily leased to middle-market companies in service-oriented or experience-based businesses. The company focuses on acquiring properties through sale-leaseback transactions and master leases, aiming for long-term, stable revenue streams. Its strategy emphasizes diversification across tenants and property types, with a focus on businesses less susceptible to e-commerce disruption, such as car washes, medical services, and quick-service restaurants.
