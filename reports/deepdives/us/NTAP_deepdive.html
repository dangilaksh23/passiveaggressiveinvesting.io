NetApp is a global leader in Intelligent Data Infrastructure, providing software-driven, cloud-centric data management solutions. The company's flagship ONTAP software and a portfolio of flash and cloud-native solutions enable enterprises to manage, protect, and leverage data across on-premises, hybrid, and multi-cloud environments. NetApp differentiates itself through deep cloud integration with major hyperscalers, proven data management leadership, comprehensive security, and strong ecosystem partnerships, positioning itself to help customers harness data for digital transformation and the rise of AI.
