Business Model · 10-K 2025-11

Universal Technical Institute (UTI) is a leading provider of workforce education in the transportation, skilled trades, energy, and healthcare sectors. The company offers hands-on training programs through its Universal Technical Institute (UTI) and Concorde Career Colleges divisions, preparing students for in-demand careers. UTI partners with industry manufacturers and healthcare facilities to ensure its curricula meet employer needs, aiming to provide graduates with enhanced employment opportunities and higher earning potential.
