Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Texas Pacific Land Corporation is a major landowner in Texas, primarily in the Permian Basin, holding vast surface acreage and significant oil and gas royalty interests. The company generates revenue by leasing its land for infrastructure development, providing materials and water for oil and gas operations, and collecting royalties from oil and gas production. Its strategy focuses on optimizing these legacy assets while exploring new opportunities in renewable energy and data infrastructure, emphasizing high cash flow margins and low capital expenditures.
