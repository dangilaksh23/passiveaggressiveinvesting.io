Rush Street Interactive is a leading online gaming and entertainment company operating in the U.S., Canada, and Latin America. The company offers real-money online casino, online sports betting, and retail sports betting, alongside social gaming. It generates revenue primarily through a business-to-consumer (B2C) model, offering its services directly to end customers via its proprietary platform and brands like BetRivers and PlaySugarHouse. RSI differentiates itself through its proprietary, customizable gaming platform, unique product offerings including self-developed casino games, and a focus on customer engagement via its iRush Rewards loyalty program.
