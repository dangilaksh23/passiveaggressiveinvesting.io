Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

O'Reilly Automotive is a major specialty retailer of automotive aftermarket parts, tools, supplies, equipment, and accessories across North America. The company serves both do-it-yourself (DIY) customers and professional service providers through a dual market strategy, operating over 6,400 stores in the US, Mexico, and Canada. O'Reilly differentiates itself through superior customer service, technically proficient staff, a strategic distribution network, and a broad product selection, aiming to be the dominant auto parts provider in its markets.
