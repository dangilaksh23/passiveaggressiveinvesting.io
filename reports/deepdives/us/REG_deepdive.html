Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

This company is a Real Estate Investment Trust (REIT) that generates revenue primarily from rental income and property operations. It owns and operates a portfolio of shopping centers, deriving income from base rent, recoveries from tenants, and percentage rent. The company's competitive advantage appears to stem from its scale and the contractual nature of its lease agreements, which provide predictable revenue streams.
