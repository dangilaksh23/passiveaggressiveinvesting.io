Business Model · 10-K 2026-06

Nathan's Famous is a well-established brand primarily known for its hot dogs and other food products, operating through a multi-channel business model. The company generates revenue through licensing its brand for retail products, selling its branded products to foodservice operators, and operating franchised restaurants. Its competitive distinction lies in its iconic brand recognition, a proprietary recipe for its premium hot dogs, and a flexible business model that maximizes distribution points across various retail and foodservice formats.
