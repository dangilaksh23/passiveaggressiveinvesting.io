Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Bentley Systems is an infrastructure engineering software company that develops and supports a comprehensive portfolio of integrated software offerings. The company's software enables users to design, build, and operate infrastructure assets across various sectors, including public works/utilities, resources, industrial, and commercial/facilities. Bentley Systems makes money by selling its software products and cloud-connected services, aiming to improve project delivery and asset performance through digital workflows and digital twins.
