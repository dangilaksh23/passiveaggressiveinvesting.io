Business Model · 10-K 2025-09

Copart operates as a global online vehicle remarketing service, primarily facilitating the sale of vehicles through internet auctions. The company generates revenue by charging fees to sellers for its remarketing services, including auction fees, transportation, and storage. Copart's competitive advantage lies in its proprietary Virtual Bidding Third Generation (VB3) technology, which provides a global online auction platform, increasing buyer participation and operational efficiency.
