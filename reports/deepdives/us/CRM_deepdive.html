Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

Salesforce is a global leader in customer relationship management (CRM) technology, providing a unified platform that integrates sales, service, marketing, commerce, and collaboration tools. The company leverages AI-powered agents and a comprehensive platform to help organizations become "agentic enterprises," enabling them to unify customer data, automate workflows, and deliver personalized experiences. Salesforce primarily generates revenue through subscriptions for its flexible and scalable cloud-based services, sold directly and through partners, aiming to increase customer productivity and drive growth.
