Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Lantheus Holdings is a radiopharmaceutical company that develops, manufactures, and commercializes innovative imaging agents and radiopharmaceutical drugs. They generate revenue by selling these products, primarily for diagnostic imaging and oncology treatments, to hospitals and imaging centers. The company differentiates itself through its established distribution network, efficient manufacturing processes, and a growing portfolio of specialized products.
