Business Model · 10-K 2025-08

Parker-Hannifin Corporation is a global leader in motion and control technologies, designing, manufacturing, and supporting highly engineered solutions. The company serves diverse markets including aerospace, defense, industrial equipment, transportation, energy, and HVAC/refrigeration. Parker differentiates itself through a unique combination of interconnected technologies, a decentralized operating structure fostering customer connections, and a business system called "The Win Strategy" focused on engaged people, customer experience, and profitable growth.
