Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Veralto Corporation provides essential technology solutions focused on safeguarding vital resources like water and ensuring product quality and safety. The company operates through two segments: Water Quality, offering analytics and treatment solutions for municipal and industrial clients, and Product Quality Innovation, providing marking, coding, and color management tools for consumer packaged goods and pharmaceuticals. Veralto differentiates itself through strong global brands, a legacy of innovation, deep customer relationships, and a proprietary business management system (VES) that drives continuous improvement and operational excellence.
