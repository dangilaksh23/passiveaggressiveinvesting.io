Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

AppFolio provides a cloud-based platform for property management companies, offering a comprehensive suite of tools to manage various property types. The platform centralizes operational and financial data, automates workflows, and facilitates transactions across the real estate lifecycle, including payments, tenant screening, and risk mitigation. Its competitive distinction lies in its AI-powered features, extensible marketplace for third-party integrations, and a customer-centric approach aimed at improving efficiency and customer experience.
