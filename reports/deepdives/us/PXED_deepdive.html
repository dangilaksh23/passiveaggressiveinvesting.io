Business Model · 10-K 2025-11

Phoenix Education Partners, Inc. operates as a mission-driven organization in the post-secondary education market, focusing on providing accessible, career-relevant higher education to adult learners. The company leverages flexible, asynchronous learning models and technology-driven platforms to help students achieve their professional goals and employers upskill their workforce. Its competitive distinction lies in its tailored approach to adult learners, who face unique challenges, and its strong focus on employer relationships for B2B enrollment.
