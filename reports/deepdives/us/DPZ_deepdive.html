Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Domino's is the world's largest pizza company, operating over 22,100 locations across more than 90 markets. The company primarily functions as a franchisor, generating revenue through royalties and fees from its independent franchisees, who own and operate approximately 99% of its global stores. Domino's focuses on delivering quality, affordable pizza and other food items through both delivery and carryout service models, leveraging its strong global brand and technological innovations to maintain a competitive edge.
