Business Model · 10-K 2026-01

Innovative Designs, Inc. produces and sells a house wrap product called Insultex, which has thermal resistance and buoyancy properties. The company also previously offered a cold weather apparel line, Arctic Armor, but is now only selling existing inventory. Their primary revenue driver is the Insultex House Wrap, with a smaller portion from remaining Arctic Armor products. The company aims to rebuild its House Wrap brand and eventually produce its own Insultex material.
