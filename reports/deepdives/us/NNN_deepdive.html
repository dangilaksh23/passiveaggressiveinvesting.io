Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

NNN REIT, Inc. is a fully integrated real estate investment trust that acquires, owns, invests in, and develops high-quality properties leased primarily to tenants under long-term, net leases. The company focuses on single-tenant, freestanding net lease properties, aiming for stable current returns and potential capital appreciation. Its competitive distinction lies in its long history of consecutive annual dividend increases and its focus on properties with minimal ongoing capital expenditures, providing attractive opportunities for stable income and growth.
