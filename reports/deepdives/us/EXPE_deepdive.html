Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Expedia Group operates as a global travel marketplace, connecting travelers with a vast array of travel products and services through its portfolio of trusted brands. The company generates revenue by facilitating bookings for lodging, flights, car rentals, and activities, acting as both a merchant and an agent. Its competitive distinction lies in its extensive supply network, proprietary technology, and data-driven approach to delivering personalized travel experiences, aiming to simplify and enhance the entire travel journey for consumers.
