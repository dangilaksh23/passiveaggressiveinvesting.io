Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

VOC Energy Trust is a statutory trust formed to hold a net profits interest in oil and natural gas properties located in Kansas and Texas. The Trust receives 80% of the net proceeds from the sale and production of these properties, which are operated by third-party entities. Its revenue is directly tied to oil and natural gas sales prices and production volumes, with a finite life based on production depletion and a set termination date. Distributions to unitholders represent a return of investment and are made quarterly after deducting administrative expenses.
