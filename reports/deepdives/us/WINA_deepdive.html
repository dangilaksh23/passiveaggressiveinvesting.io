Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Winmark Corporation is a franchisor focused on sustainability and small business formation, operating five award-winning resale franchise brands: Plato's Closet, Once Upon A Child, Play It Again Sports, Style Encore, and Music Go Round. The company generates revenue primarily through royalties and franchise fees from its franchisees who buy and sell gently used and some new merchandise, offering consumers value and a sustainable way to recycle goods. Winmark differentiates itself by championing entrepreneurs and providing them with the technology, tools, and training to operate successful multi-channel retail businesses.
