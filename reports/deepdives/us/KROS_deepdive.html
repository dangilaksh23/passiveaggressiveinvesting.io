Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

KROS is a clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company focused on developing novel therapeutics for disorders linked to dysfunctional signaling of the transforming growth factor-beta (TGF-β) protein family. They leverage their understanding of TGF-β's role in tissue growth, repair, and maintenance to create protein therapeutics. Their lead candidates, rinvatercept and elritercept, target neuromuscular diseases and cytopenias in blood disorders, respectively, aiming to provide disease-modifying benefits to patients with limited treatment options.
