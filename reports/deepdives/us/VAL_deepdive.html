Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Valaris is a global offshore contract drilling company providing services to international oil and gas companies. It operates a large fleet of offshore drilling rigs, including ultra-deepwater and premium jackup rigs, across major offshore markets worldwide. The company generates revenue by providing integrated drilling services on a day rate contract basis, where customers bear the primary costs and risks associated with well construction and success. Valaris distinguishes itself through its extensive and high-specification rig fleet and its broad geographic diversification.
