Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Curbline Properties Corp. is a real estate investment trust (REIT) that owns and operates a portfolio of convenience retail properties. The company generates revenue primarily through rental income from its tenants, which are typically businesses operating within these retail centers. Curbline aims to provide essential goods and services to local communities, and its competitive advantage lies in its portfolio of well-located properties and its focus on convenience-oriented retail. The company was spun off from SITE Centers Corp. in October 2024, contributing 79 convenience properties to its portfolio.
