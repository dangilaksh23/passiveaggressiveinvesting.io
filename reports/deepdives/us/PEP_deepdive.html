Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

PepsiCo is a leading global company that produces and sells a wide range of beverages and convenient food products. It operates through a diversified portfolio of well-known brands such as Lay's, Doritos, Gatorade, Pepsi-Cola, and Quaker. The company generates revenue by manufacturing, marketing, and distributing these products to customers and consumers in over 200 countries and territories, leveraging a combination of direct-to-consumer sales, distributor networks, and e-commerce platforms. Its competitive distinction lies in its extensive brand recognition, broad product portfolio, and established global distribution network.
