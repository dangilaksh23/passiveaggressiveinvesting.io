Doximity operates as a physician-first technology company, providing a professional platform for over 3 million registered U.S. medical professionals. The company offers AI-powered tools for collaboration, staying updated on medical news, career management, and virtual patient visits. Revenue is generated through Marketing, Hiring, and Workflow Solutions sold primarily to pharmaceutical manufacturers and health systems, leveraging the platform's extensive network and data insights.
