Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Virtu Financial is a global financial firm that provides liquidity and trading solutions to clients worldwide using advanced technology. The company operates in two segments: Market Making and Execution Services, generating revenue by facilitating trades across various asset classes like equities, ETFs, options, forex, and cryptocurrencies. Its competitive advantage stems from its proprietary, scalable technology platform, low-cost structure, and extensive global market access, enabling efficient risk transfer and improved market transparency for participants.
