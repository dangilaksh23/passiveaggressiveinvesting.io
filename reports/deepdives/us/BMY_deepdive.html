Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Bristol Myers Squibb is a biopharmaceutical company focused on discovering, developing, and delivering innovative medicines to patients facing serious diseases. They operate globally, combining the scale of a large pharmaceutical company with the agility of a biotech firm. Their strategy centers on transformational medicines in areas like oncology, hematology, and immunology, aiming for operational excellence and strategic capital allocation for long-term growth.
