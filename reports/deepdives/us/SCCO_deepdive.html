Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Southern Copper Corporation is a major integrated producer of copper, molybdenum, zinc, and silver, with operations primarily located in Peru and Mexico. The company extracts, smelts, and refines these metals, generating revenue from their sale on global commodity markets. Its competitive distinction lies in its significant scale, extensive copper reserves, and a high degree of vertical integration across its mining and processing facilities.
