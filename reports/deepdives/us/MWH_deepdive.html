Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

SOLV Energy is a leading provider of integrated infrastructure services for the power industry, specializing in utility-scale solar and battery storage projects. The company offers a full lifecycle approach, encompassing engineering, procurement, construction (EPC), and ongoing operations and maintenance (O&M) services. This comprehensive offering allows them to build and then manage power plants, leveraging operational data to enhance future project designs and customer value, distinguishing them from competitors focused solely on construction or O&M.
