Business Model · 10-K 2025-08

Brinker International, Inc. operates and franchises two distinct casual dining restaurant brands: Chili's Grill & Bar and Maggiano's Little Italy. Chili's offers a value-driven, casual dining experience with Southwest-inspired American cuisine, known for burgers, fajitas, and margaritas. Maggiano's provides a polished casual Italian-American dining experience, focusing on special occasions and large parties with abundant portions. The company generates revenue through food and beverage sales at its company-owned restaurants and royalties from its franchisees, aiming to grow sales and profitability by enhancing guest experience, simplifying operations, and expanding its digital and off-premise offerings.

Key Risks
△ Inability to successfully design and execute business strategies may adversely affect sales and profitability.
△ Changes in consumer preferences, including health and dietary trends, could decrease demand for restaurant offerings.
△ Food safety incidents at company-owned or franchised restaurants or in the supply chain could damage brand reputation and reduce sales.
△ Unfavorable publicity, especially concerning the Chili's brand, can negatively impact public perception and financial performance.
△ Reliance on digital ordering and third-party delivery providers introduces risks of platform interruptions and service quality issues.
