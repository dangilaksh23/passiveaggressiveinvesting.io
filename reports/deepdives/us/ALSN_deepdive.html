Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Allison Transmission is a global leader in designing, manufacturing, and distributing advanced propulsion solutions, primarily fully automatic transmissions for medium- and heavy-duty commercial vehicles and defense applications. The company also offers solutions for off-highway and electric hybrid/electric vehicles. Allison differentiates itself through its long-standing brand reputation for performance, reliability, and fuel efficiency, supported by a broad product portfolio and a global distribution network.
