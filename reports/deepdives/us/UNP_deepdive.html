Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Union Pacific Corporation, through its principal operating company Union Pacific Railroad Company, is a major freight railroad company connecting 23 states in the western two-thirds of the United States. It provides critical logistics services for a diversified mix of bulk, industrial, and premium freight, linking major West Coast and Gulf Coast ports to Eastern gateways and connecting with Canada and Mexico. The company's competitive distinction lies in its extensive network, serving key growth centers and international trade routes, while emphasizing safety, service, and operational excellence to drive freight volume growth.
