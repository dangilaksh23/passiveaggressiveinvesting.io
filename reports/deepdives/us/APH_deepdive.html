Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Amphenol Corporation is a leading global designer, manufacturer, and marketer of electrical, electronic, and fiber optic connectors and interconnect systems, antennas, sensors, and related products. The company serves a diverse range of end markets including automotive, aerospace, communications networks, defense, industrial, and information technology. Amphenol differentiates itself through comprehensive design capabilities, a broad product selection, high quality and service, and a focus on technological leadership in key interconnect areas, supported by a strategy of market diversification and global expansion.
