Business Model · 10-K 2026-04

AZZ Inc. is a North American provider of metal coating solutions, offering hot-dip galvanizing and coil coating services. The company operates through three segments: Metal Coatings, Precoat Metals, and a stake in Infrastructure Solutions. AZZ's services protect and enhance the appearance and lifecycle of steel and aluminum products used in construction, appliances, HVAC, and infrastructure, aiming to deliver sustainable solutions that reduce emissions and extend product life.
