Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

Westlake Chemical Partners LP is a Delaware limited partnership that operates and develops ethylene production facilities and related assets. The company generates revenue primarily by selling ethylene and co-products produced by its subsidiary, OpCo, through a long-term, fee-based ethylene sales agreement with Westlake. Its competitive distinction lies in its ownership and control of significant ethylene production capacity and a dedicated pipeline, integrated with Westlake's broader operations.
