Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

OppFi is a tech-enabled specialty finance platform that partners with community banks to provide credit access to "everyday Americans" who are often turned away by mainstream financial institutions due to factors like low credit scores. The company's primary product, OppLoans, offers transparent, responsible installment loans with flexible repayment options and reports payments to credit bureaus, aiming to improve customers' financial health. OppFi differentiates itself through its highly automated, digital platform, proprietary underwriting algorithms, and a strong focus on customer experience and financial education.
