Business Model · 10-K 2025-08

The Clorox Company is a global manufacturer and marketer of consumer and professional products, generating $7.1 billion in net sales. It operates across approximately 25 countries, selling through various retail channels and e-commerce. The company is known for its strong portfolio of trusted brands, including Clorox, Glad, Brita, and Burt's Bees, many of which hold leading market share positions. Clorox focuses on innovation and sustainability through its IGNITE strategy to drive profitable growth and deliver value to stakeholders.
