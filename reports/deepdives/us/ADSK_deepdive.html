Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

Autodesk is a global leader in 3D design, engineering, and entertainment technology solutions, serving industries like architecture, engineering, construction, manufacturing, and media. The company generates revenue by selling professional software products through direct and indirect channels, enabling customers to visualize, simulate, and analyze real-world performance early in the design process. Autodesk's competitive distinction lies in its comprehensive portfolio of integrated software and cloud-based platforms that foster innovation, optimize designs, and streamline complex workflows across various industries.
