02The Financial Picture

TRPCF's financial performance in FY2025 is nothing short of impressive. This growth is paired with exceptional profitability, with an operating margin of 27.4% and a free cash flow (FCF) margin of 21.8%, both ranking in the top percentiles of its sector and showing an improving trend.

Furthermore, the balance sheet is pristine, with a net debt/EBITDA ratio of -0.2x, indicating a net cash position. Return on Equity (ROE) is solid at 19.5% and trending upwards, reinforcing the picture of a highly efficient and financially healthy enterprise. At a trailing PE of 8.4 and Price/Book of 1.56, these metrics appear quite attractive for such a high-quality, growing business.
