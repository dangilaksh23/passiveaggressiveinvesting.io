Business Model · 10-K 2025-11

Fair Isaac Corporation (FICO) is a global analytics software leader founded in 1956. The company operationalizes data analytics through its widely used FICO Score and software solutions, enabling businesses in over 80 countries to improve decision-making. FICO serves leading financial institutions, insurers, retailers, and telecommunications providers, and also offers direct-to-consumer services to enhance financial literacy. Its competitive distinction lies in its long-standing expertise in predictive modeling and decision management, making its FICO Score the standard measure of consumer credit risk in the U.S.
