Business Model · 10-K 2025-06

Earth Science Tech, Inc. is a strategic holding company that operates a diversified portfolio of businesses focused on pharmaceutical compounding, telemedicine, and real estate development. The company generates revenue through the sale of compounded medications, telehealth consultations, and real estate transactions. Its competitive distinction lies in its integrated approach, leveraging its pharmacy and telehealth platforms to offer specialized compounded medications and services, supported by a growing real estate division and a direct-to-consumer product brand.
