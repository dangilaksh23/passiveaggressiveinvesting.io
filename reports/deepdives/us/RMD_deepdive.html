Business Model · 10-K 2025-08

ResMed is a global leader in digital health and cloud-connected medical devices, focusing on innovative solutions for sleep apnea, COPD, and other chronic diseases. The company designs and sells devices, masks, and software that help patients manage their conditions at home, aiming to improve quality of life, reduce disease impact, and lower healthcare costs. ResMed's competitive advantage lies in its early innovation in CPAP technology, its comprehensive suite of connected care solutions, and its expanding software platforms for residential care providers.
