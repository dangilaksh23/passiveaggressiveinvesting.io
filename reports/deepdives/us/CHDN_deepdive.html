Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Churchill Downs Incorporated (CDI) is a diversified entertainment company with a long history, most notably as the home of the Kentucky Derby. The company operates across three main segments: Live and Historical Racing, Wagering Services and Solutions, and Gaming. CDI generates revenue through pari-mutuel wagering commissions, simulcast fees, event-related services, food and beverage sales at its racing venues, and operations of regional casino gaming properties. Its competitive distinction lies in its iconic brand, particularly the Kentucky Derby, and its expanding portfolio of historical racing machines and casino properties.
