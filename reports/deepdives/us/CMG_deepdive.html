Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Chipotle Mexican Grill operates and owns a chain of fast-casual restaurants specializing in burritos, burrito bowls, quesadillas, tacos, and salads. The company focuses on serving real food with wholesome ingredients, emphasizing responsibly sourced and classically cooked items without artificial additives. Chipotle aims to provide a great guest experience and make its food accessible, differentiating itself through its commitment to "Food with Integrity" and a strategy focused on operational excellence, menu innovation, technology modernization, global expansion, and talent development.
