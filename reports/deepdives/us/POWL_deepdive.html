Business Model · 10-K 2025-11

Powell Industries designs, manufactures, and services custom-engineered electrical equipment and systems for distributing, controlling, and monitoring electrical energy. The company primarily serves the oil and gas, petrochemical, and electric utility markets, offering solutions like integrated power control room substations and switchgear. Its competitive advantage lies in its expertise in custom engineering, project management, and systems integration, enabling it to deliver tailored solutions for complex customer projects.
