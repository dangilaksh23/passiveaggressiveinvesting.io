Business Model · 10-K 2025-10

AutoZone is a leading retailer and distributor of automotive replacement parts and accessories across the Americas. The company operates a vast network of physical stores and also sells products online, serving both individual consumers and commercial repair businesses. AutoZone differentiates itself through its extensive product line, a commercial sales program offering prompt delivery and credit, and a strong focus on customer service and employee culture.
