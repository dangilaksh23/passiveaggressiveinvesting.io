Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

W. P. Carey is a diversified real estate investment trust (REIT) that owns and manages a large portfolio of commercial properties, primarily industrial, warehouse, and retail facilities, net-leased to companies on long-term contracts. The company generates most of its revenue from lease payments, focusing on sale-leaseback transactions where it acquires a company's critical real estate and leases it back. Its strategy emphasizes stable cash flow generation through diversified, high-quality assets with built-in rent escalators, aiming to grow dividends and stockholder value.
