Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Sterling Infrastructure, Inc. is a diversified infrastructure solutions provider operating across three segments: E-Infrastructure, Transportation, and Building Solutions in the United States. The company generates revenue by undertaking large-scale site development, mission-critical electrical services, transportation infrastructure projects, and residential and commercial concrete construction. Sterling differentiates itself through its focus on higher-margin projects, strong project management capabilities, and a strategic shift away from low-bid heavy highway work towards more specialized and profitable offerings.
