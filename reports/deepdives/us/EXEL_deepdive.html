Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Exelixis is an oncology company focused on developing and commercializing innovative cancer medicines. The company's primary revenue driver is cabozantinib, a tyrosine kinase inhibitor approved for various forms of cancer, including renal cell carcinoma and thyroid cancer. Exelixis also has a pipeline of novel drug candidates, including zanzalintinib, and leverages strategic partnerships for global commercialization. Its competitive advantage lies in its established oncology products, ongoing clinical development, and a disciplined approach to investment in scientific innovation.
