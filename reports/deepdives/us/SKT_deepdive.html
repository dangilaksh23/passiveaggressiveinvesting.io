Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Tanger Inc. is a leading owner and operator of outlet and open-air retail destinations in the United States and Canada. The company operates as a self-administered REIT, focusing on developing, acquiring, and managing these retail centers. Tanger generates revenue primarily through rental income from a diverse base of over 700 brand name companies operating more than 2,600 stores across its portfolio, which is known for offering branded products at discounted prices.
