Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Comfort Systems USA provides comprehensive mechanical and electrical contracting services, focusing on HVAC, plumbing, piping, controls, and electrical system installation and servicing. The company operates through a network of 50 units across 142 locations in the United States, primarily serving commercial, industrial, and institutional markets. Their revenue is generated from both new construction installations and renovation, maintenance, repair, and replacement services in existing buildings, with a strategic emphasis on design-build projects and energy-efficient solutions.
