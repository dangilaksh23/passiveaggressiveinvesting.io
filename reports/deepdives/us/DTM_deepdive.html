Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

DT Midstream is an owner, operator, and developer of integrated natural gas midstream assets, providing services through interstate and intrastate pipelines, storage systems, and gathering infrastructure. The company connects premium natural gas production areas in the Marcellus/Utica and Haynesville formations to key demand centers and LNG export terminals. Its business model is characterized by stable, long-term growth driven by contractual cash flows from a diverse customer base including producers, local distribution companies, and power generators.
