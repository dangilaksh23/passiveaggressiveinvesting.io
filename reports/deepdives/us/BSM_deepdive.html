Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Black Stone Minerals, L.P. is a publicly traded partnership that owns and manages oil and natural gas mineral and royalty interests. The company generates revenue through lease bonuses, royalties, and other interests derived from the production of hydrocarbons on its properties. Its competitive advantage lies in its extensive portfolio of mineral and royalty assets, which provide a diversified and long-term revenue stream without the direct operational risks and capital expenditures associated with exploration and production companies.

Key Risks
△ Volatility in oil and natural gas prices significantly impacts financial condition and cash distributions.
△ Dependence on unaffiliated operators for exploration, development, and production introduces risks related to their efficiency and payments.
△ Production decline rates and the ability to replace current and future production pose a risk to the business.
△ Environmental, legal, and regulatory risks, including potential reductions in demand for oil and natural gas, compliance with laws, and climate change concerns, are significant.
△ Title defects to properties can impair the company's interests.
