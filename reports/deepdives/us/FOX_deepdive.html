Business Model · 10-K 2025-08

Fox Corporation is a leading news, sports, and entertainment company that generates revenue by producing and licensing content across various platforms, including traditional cable, broadcast television, and digital services like the AVOD platform Tubi. The company differentiates itself through its strong portfolio of premium brands, such as FOX News, FOX Sports, and FOX Entertainment, which focus on live and appointment-based content. Its extensive distribution network across major U.S. markets and its ability to secure affiliate fees and advertising sales contribute to its competitive advantage.
