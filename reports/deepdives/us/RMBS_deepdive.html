Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Rambus is a global semiconductor company that designs and provides advanced silicon chips and intellectual property (IP) for data-intensive computing systems, primarily serving the data center and AI infrastructure markets. The company generates revenue through the sale of memory interface chips, silicon IP licenses, and patent licenses. Rambus differentiates itself through its deep expertise in memory technology, innovative architectures, and a strong portfolio of patents, enabling high-performance and secure solutions for demanding AI workloads.
