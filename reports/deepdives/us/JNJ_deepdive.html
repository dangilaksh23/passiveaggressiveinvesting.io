Johnson & Johnson is a global healthcare company that develops and manufactures a wide range of products. It operates across multiple segments, including pharmaceuticals, medical devices, and consumer health. The company generates revenue through the sale of these products, aiming to improve health and well-being worldwide. Its competitive distinction lies in its broad portfolio, extensive research and development capabilities, and established global presence.
