Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

WisdomTree is a global financial innovator that offers a diverse suite of exchange-traded products (ETPs), models, private market investments, and digital asset-related products. The company aims to empower investors and equip financial professionals by creating transparent and accessible financial products, leveraging advanced financial infrastructure. WisdomTree is expanding beyond its core ETP business into next-generation digital products like tokenized real-world assets and stablecoins, as well as private assets through acquisitions, managing approximately $144.5 billion in assets under management as of December 31, 2025.
