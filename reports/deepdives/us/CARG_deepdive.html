Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

CarGurus operates a multinational automotive platform that connects consumers and dealers for buying and selling vehicles. It aims to bring trust and transparency to car shopping by providing a large selection of inventory, trusted insights, and data-driven products. The company offers dealers software solutions to improve efficiency and profitability in inventory acquisition, pricing, marketing, and sales, while empowering consumers throughout their shopping journey.
