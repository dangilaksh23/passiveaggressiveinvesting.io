Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Armstrong World Industries designs and manufactures innovative architectural applications for interiors and exteriors, including ceilings, specialty walls, and exterior metal solutions. The company generates revenue by selling these products, which are made from a variety of materials like mineral fiber, fiberglass, metal, and wood, to distributors, contractors, and retailers. Armstrong differentiates itself through a strong brand, a broad product portfolio, design capabilities, and a focus on product innovation for both new construction and renovation projects.
