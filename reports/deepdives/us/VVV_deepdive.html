Business Model · 10-K 2025-11

Valvoline Inc. is a leading provider of automotive preventive maintenance services, operating and franchising approximately 2,200 quick-lube service centers across the U.S. and Canada. The company offers convenient, 15-minute "stay-in-your-car" oil changes, battery services, tire rotations, and other manufacturer-recommended maintenance. Valvoline differentiates itself through its established brand, a consistent service model utilizing proprietary technology, and a focus on customer satisfaction, aiming to simplify vehicle care for consumers and commercial clients.
