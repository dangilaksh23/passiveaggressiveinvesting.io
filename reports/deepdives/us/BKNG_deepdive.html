Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Booking Holdings operates as a global online travel reservation service, connecting consumers with travel service providers across accommodations, flights, ground transportation, activities, and restaurants. The company generates revenue primarily through commissions on bookings facilitated through its various brands, including Booking.com, Priceline, Agoda, KAYAK, and OpenTable. Its competitive distinction lies in its extensive brand portfolio, a robust loyalty program (Genius), and a strategic focus on integrating generative AI to enhance user experience and operational efficiencies, aiming to simplify and personalize the entire travel journey.

△ Declines or disruptions in the travel industry due to economic conditions, geopolitical tensions, or unforeseen events like pandemics or natural disasters can adversely affect financial performance.
△ Intense competition from online travel companies, direct booking channels, and large technology companies leveraging AI could reduce market share and harm financial performance.
