Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

IDEXX Laboratories develops, manufactures, and distributes diagnostic products and services for the veterinary, livestock, poultry, dairy, and water testing industries. They also produce human medical point-of-care diagnostic products. The company aims to enhance the health and well-being of animals and people through its comprehensive diagnostic solutions, which include point-of-care instruments, consumables, reference laboratory services, and practice management software. IDEXX differentiates itself through integrated information management technologies, proprietary AI capabilities in its analyzers, and a broad range of diagnostic tests designed for speed, accuracy, and ease of use.
