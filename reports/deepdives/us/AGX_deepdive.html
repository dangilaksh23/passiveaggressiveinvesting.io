Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

Argan, Inc. is a holding company that operates primarily as an engineering and construction firm through its subsidiaries. It provides a comprehensive range of services including engineering, procurement, construction, commissioning, and maintenance across three segments: Power, Industrial, and Teledata. The company generates revenue by undertaking large-scale energy and industrial projects, with a significant focus on the power generation market. Its competitive distinction lies in its proven track record, experienced teams, and ability to manage complex technical and scheduling challenges for project owners.
