Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

Xponential Fitness is a leading global franchisor of boutique health and wellness brands, operating a diversified platform of five brands including Pilates, barre, stretching, functional training, and yoga. The company partners with franchisees to offer accessible, personalized workout experiences in studio locations across North America and internationally. Its competitive distinction lies in its scaled multi-brand offering, resilient franchise model with strong unit economics, and an integrated platform that supports franchisee growth and enhances member experience through data and innovation.
