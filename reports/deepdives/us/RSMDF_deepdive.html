ResMed is a global leader in digital health and cloud-connected medical devices, focusing on treating sleep apnea, COPD, and other chronic diseases. The company designs innovative solutions that empower patients to live healthier lives outside of the hospital, improving quality of life and reducing healthcare costs. Their offerings include CPAP systems, ventilation devices, diagnostic products, and comprehensive residential care software platforms that support healthcare professionals and caregivers.
