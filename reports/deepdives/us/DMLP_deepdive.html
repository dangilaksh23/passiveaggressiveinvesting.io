Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Dorchester Minerals, L.P. is a limited partnership that owns and administers royalty and net profits interests in oil and natural gas properties across the United States. The company generates revenue primarily from these interests, receiving a share of the net profits or production from properties operated by third parties. Its business objective is to provide an attractive yield to unitholders by strategically managing its assets, minimizing costs, and seeking accretive acquisitions, while maintaining a conservative, debt-free capital structure.
