McDonald's Corporation is a global leader in the quick-service restaurant industry, operating and franchising McDonald's restaurants. The company generates revenue primarily through rent and royalties paid by franchisees, as well as through company-operated restaurants. Its competitive distinction lies in its globally recognized brand, extensive supply chain, and operational efficiency, allowing it to serve millions of customers daily.
