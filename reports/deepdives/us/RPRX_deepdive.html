Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Royalty Pharma is the largest buyer of biopharmaceutical royalties and a leading funder of innovation in the life sciences industry. The company generates revenue by acquiring rights to receive payments based on the top-line sales of approved biopharmaceutical products and development-stage product candidates. Its competitive advantage stems from its scale, deep life sciences expertise, extensive industry relationships, and a rigorous, data-driven investment process, allowing it to selectively acquire royalties on high-quality therapies addressing significant unmet patient needs.
