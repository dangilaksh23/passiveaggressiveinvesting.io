Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

The Coca-Cola Company is a global total beverage company that owns or licenses and markets a wide array of beverage brands across categories like sparkling flavors, water, sports, coffee, tea, and juices. It generates revenue by selling beverage concentrates and syrups to a network of independent bottling partners and distributors, who then produce and sell finished beverages to retailers and consumers worldwide. The company's competitive distinction lies in its portfolio of globally recognized, loved brands and its extensive distribution network, aiming to connect with consumers through diverse beverage options and sustainable practices.
