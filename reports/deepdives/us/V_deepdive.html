Business Model · 10-K 2025-11

Visa is a global leader in digital payments, facilitating secure and efficient money movement for consumers, businesses, and governments. The company operates a four-party model, connecting consumers, issuing and acquiring financial institutions, and sellers through its proprietary VisaNet network. Visa generates revenue by processing transactions and offering a suite of value-added services, aiming to expand digital payment adoption and innovate with new technologies like AI and stablecoins.
