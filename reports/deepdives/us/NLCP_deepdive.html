Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

NewLake Capital Partners is a real estate investment trust (REIT) that provides essential real estate capital to state-licensed cannabis operators. The company focuses on sale-leaseback transactions, third-party purchases, and funding for build-to-suit projects, primarily for cultivation and dispensing facilities. By offering financing solutions in an industry underserved by traditional banking due to federal regulations, NewLake aims to generate stable rental income and potential property appreciation.

Key Risks
△ Dependence on state-licensed cannabis operators whose financial stability is tied to a rapidly evolving and federally illegal industry.
△ Potential for increased competition from other real estate capital providers and traditional financing sources as the cannabis industry matures.
△ Risk of tenant default, which could impact rental income and property value.
△ Changes in federal or state laws and regulations governing cannabis could alter market dynamics and financing options.
△ The specialized nature of cannabis real estate may limit remarketing options if a tenant defaults.
