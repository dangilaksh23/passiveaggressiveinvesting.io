Business Model · 10-K 2026-05

Gen Digital Inc. is a global leader in consumer Cyber Safety and Trust-Based Solutions, offering a suite of products and services designed to protect users' data, identity, privacy, reputation, and financial well-being. Through its well-known brands like Norton, Avast, and LifeLock, the company provides cybersecurity, online privacy, and identity protection solutions. It also offers financial wellness tools and insights, recognizing the increasing intersection of digital and financial lives. Gen aims to empower individuals and small businesses to navigate an evolving digital landscape, leveraging technology and a diverse go-to-market strategy to drive growth and customer engagement.
