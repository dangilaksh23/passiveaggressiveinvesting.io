Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

LTC Properties, Inc. is a real estate investment trust (REIT) that primarily invests in seniors housing and health care properties. The company generates revenue through rental income from triple net leases, resident fees, interest on financing receivables and mortgage loans, and income from joint ventures. LTC Properties differentiates itself by focusing on newer properties with strong cash flows and experienced operators, and by employing a strategy of sourcing single property or small portfolio transactions that are not broadly marketed.

Key Risks
△ Financial performance is impacted by operational and legal risks under the new SHOP segment.
△ Dependence on third-party SHOP operators for successful management and operation of communities.
△ Financial condition and ability to pay dividends could be adversely affected by financial or operational difficulties of operators, lessees, or borrowers.
△ Subject to risks associated with public health crises, including pandemics, which can impact occupancy and operating costs.
△ The health care industry is heavily regulated, and changes in laws could impose negative costs and restrictions.
