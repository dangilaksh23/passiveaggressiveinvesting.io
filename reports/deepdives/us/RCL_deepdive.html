Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Royal Caribbean Group is a leader in the vacation industry, operating three global cruise brands: Royal Caribbean, Celebrity Cruises, and Silversea, along with a 50% joint venture interest in German brands TUI Cruises and Hapag-Lloyd Cruises. The company offers a wide range of worldwide cruise itineraries, calling on over 1,000 destinations across all seven continents, and also owns private land-based destinations. Its competitive advantage is built on innovation, fleet quality, exceptional service, unique destinations, and leveraging technology to provide memorable vacation experiences.
