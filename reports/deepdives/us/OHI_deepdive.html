Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Omega Healthcare Investors, Inc. is a real estate investment trust (REIT) that invests in healthcare-related properties across the U.S., U.K., and Canada. The company primarily owns and leases skilled nursing facilities (SNFs), assisted living facilities (ALFs), and other specialized healthcare properties to third-party operators. Its objective is to provide strong investor returns by serving as a capital partner to healthcare operators, enabling them to focus on patient care.

Key Risks
△ Operators may not meet their payment obligations, impacting yields.
△ Reliance on third-party property managers for RIDEA structures, with limited control over operational risks.
△ Potential for liabilities related to personal injury, quality of care claims, and governmental reimbursement matters in RIDEA facilities.
△ The long-term care industry is evolving, requiring continuous evaluation of assets and operators.
△ Competitive markets for healthcare real estate acquisitions.
Key Initiatives

→ Pursuing investments in alternative jurisdictions outside of the U.S. and U.K.
→ Making select ancillary investments in companies that enhance technology and infrastructure for long-term care providers.
