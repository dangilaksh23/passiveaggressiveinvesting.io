Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

CareTrust REIT is a self-administered real estate investment trust (REIT) that owns, acquires, finances, develops, and leases healthcare-related properties, primarily skilled nursing facilities and senior housing communities. The company generates revenue by leasing these properties to independent operators under triple-net lease arrangements, where tenants are responsible for property expenses. CareTrust also engages in other real estate-related investments, including loans and preferred equity, and has recently expanded into operating senior housing communities through a RIDEA structure, allowing it to participate directly in operational financial results.
