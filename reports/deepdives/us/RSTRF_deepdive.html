Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Restaurant Brands International Inc. is a global quick-service restaurant (QSR) company operating four iconic brands: Tim Hortons, Burger King, Popeyes, and Firehouse Subs. The company generates revenue primarily through franchising its restaurants, with over 95% of its 33,000+ locations worldwide being franchised. Its competitive advantage lies in the global scale and shared best practices across its diverse brand portfolio, which includes coffee and baked goods, flame-grilled burgers, Louisiana-style chicken, and premium subs, catering to various dayparts and consumer preferences.
