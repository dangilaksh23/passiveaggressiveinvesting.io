Business Model · 10-K 2025-09

ReposiTrak operates a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform focused on business-to-business (B2B) e-commerce, compliance traceability, and supply chain management. The company partners with retailers, wholesalers, distributors, and their product suppliers to manage programs like out-of-stock reduction, ensure compliance with regulatory requirements (such as FSMA 204 for food traceability), and improve product ordering and forecasting. Its competitive distinction lies in its extensive network of connected food suppliers, wholesalers, and retailers, enabling end-to-end traceability and supply chain efficiency.
