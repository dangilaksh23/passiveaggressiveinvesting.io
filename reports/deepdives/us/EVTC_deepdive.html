Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

EVERTEC is a leading transaction-processing and financial technology provider primarily serving Latin America, Puerto Rico, and the Caribbean. The company offers a comprehensive suite of services including merchant acquiring, payment processing, and business solutions for financial institutions, merchants, corporations, and government agencies. EVERTEC differentiates itself through its diversified business model, enabling it to offer a broad range of services from a single source across multiple channels and geographies, and its ability to leverage data for value-added services.
