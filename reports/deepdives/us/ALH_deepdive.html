Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

Alliance Laundry Systems is the world's largest designer and manufacturer of commercial laundry systems, serving diverse and resilient global end markets. The company leverages over 100 years of engineering excellence to produce high-quality, reliable equipment that meets the fundamental need for clean laundry in demanding applications. Their competitive distinction lies in the superior quality, durability, and reliability of their machines, which command a price premium and foster strong customer loyalty, supported by an extensive global distribution network.
