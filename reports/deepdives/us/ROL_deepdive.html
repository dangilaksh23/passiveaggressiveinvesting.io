Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Rollins is an international services company specializing in pest and wildlife control, as well as termite damage protection. They serve over two million residential and commercial customers across approximately 70 countries through a network of owned and franchised locations. The company generates revenue primarily from recurring pest prevention services, with additional income from one-time treatments and ancillary services like exclusion and moisture remediation. Rollins differentiates itself through its strong brand portfolio, extensive service network, proprietary technology for operational efficiency, and a focus on employee development and customer loyalty.
