Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Kimberly-Clark is a global company that manufactures and markets essential consumer products made from fibers and nonwovens. Its products, sold under well-known brands like Huggies, Kleenex, and Scott, cater to daily needs in categories such as Baby Child Care, Adult Care, Feminine Care, Family Care, and Professional. The company aims to deliver Better Care for a Better World through innovation, operational efficiency, and sustainable practices, holding strong market positions in approximately 70 countries.
