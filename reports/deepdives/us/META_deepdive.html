Business Model · 10-K 2026-01

Meta Platforms, Inc. is a technology company focused on building the future of human connection and the technology that enables it, primarily through its Family of Apps (Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, Threads, WhatsApp) and Reality Labs (VR/AR hardware and software). The company generates substantially all of its revenue from selling advertising placements to marketers across its apps, while Reality Labs generates revenue from hardware sales and software/content. Meta is investing heavily in AI and the metaverse, aiming to create immersive experiences and a new computing platform, which it believes will drive future monetization opportunities.
