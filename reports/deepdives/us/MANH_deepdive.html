Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Manhattan Associates develops, sells, deploys, services, and maintains software solutions for supply chain, inventory, and omnichannel operations. Their cloud-based, subscription-as-a-service (SaaS) applications help retailers, wholesalers, manufacturers, and logistics providers manage distribution, optimize transportation, and enhance customer engagement. The company differentiates itself through a versionless, highly extensible cloud architecture that provides quarterly innovation and AI-driven insights, enabling seamless updates and improved operational efficiency for its global customer base.
