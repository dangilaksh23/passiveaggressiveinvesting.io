Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

Pagaya Technologies Ltd. is a technology company that uses AI-powered underwriting to improve access to financial products for consumers. It connects financial institutions with investors, facilitating loans in personal, auto, and point-of-sale markets, as well as single-family rentals. The company's competitive advantage lies in its proprietary AI technology, extensive data network, and ability to process a high volume of loan applications, creating a flywheel effect for continuous improvement and network growth.
