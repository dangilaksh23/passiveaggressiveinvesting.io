Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Four Corners Property Trust, Inc. is a real estate investment trust (REIT) that owns, acquires, and leases properties primarily for restaurant and retail industries. The company generates revenue through net lease arrangements where tenants are responsible for property operating costs. It also operates a small number of restaurants. FCPT focuses on acquiring income-producing properties in major US markets, aiming to grow its portfolio and diversify its tenant base.
