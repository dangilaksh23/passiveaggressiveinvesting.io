Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Yum! Brands operates a global portfolio of quick-service restaurant concepts, including KFC, Taco Bell, Pizza Hut, and Habit Burger Grill. The company primarily generates revenue through franchising, with 97% of its over 63,000 restaurants worldwide operated by independent franchisees. Yum! Brands focuses on delighting customers with craveable food and distinctive experiences, operating responsibly, and leveraging its global scale and technology to serve customers everywhere.
