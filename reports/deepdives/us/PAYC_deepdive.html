Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Paycom provides a comprehensive, cloud-based Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) solution for human capital management (HCM). Their platform manages the entire employee lifecycle, from recruitment to retirement, using a single database for all HCM functions. This integrated approach aims to reduce administrative burdens for employers, increase employee productivity through self-management, and provide accurate, real-time data analytics, differentiating them from competitors who often rely on multiple integrated third-party systems.
