Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Travel + Leisure Co. is a leading leisure travel company that provides vacation experiences and travel inspiration. It operates through two main segments: Vacation Ownership, which is the world's largest vacation ownership business by number of owners and resorts, and Travel and Membership, which includes the largest exchange company and travel club services. The company generates revenue from selling vacation ownership interests, financing these sales, and providing management and exchange services, aiming to offer diverse travel products and services to a broad customer base.
