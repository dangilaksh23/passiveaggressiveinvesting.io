Business Model · 10-K 2025-09

Vail Resorts operates a portfolio of world-class mountain resorts and regional ski areas, offering a comprehensive winter and summer recreational experience. The company generates revenue primarily through the sale of lift tickets, including season passes, as well as ancillary services like ski school, equipment rentals, dining, and retail. Vail Resorts distinguishes itself through its extensive network of popular resorts, many located near major population centers, and its integrated pass products that encourage year-round engagement and loyalty.
