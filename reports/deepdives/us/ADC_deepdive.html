Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Agree Realty Corporation is a real estate investment trust (REIT) that owns and operates a portfolio of high-quality, net-lease retail properties. The company generates revenue primarily through rental income from its diverse tenant base, which includes national and regional retailers. Agree Realty focuses on acquiring and developing properties leased to investment-grade tenants, aiming for long-term, stable cash flows and capital appreciation.
