Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Republic Services is a major provider of environmental services in North America, operating a comprehensive network of collection, transfer, recycling, and disposal facilities. The company generates revenue by collecting, recycling, and disposing of waste and providing broader environmental solutions, including renewable energy projects and sustainability innovations. Its competitive advantage lies in its extensive infrastructure, vertically integrated operations, focus on customer relationships, and commitment to sustainability, which aims to drive profitable growth and expand its addressable market.
