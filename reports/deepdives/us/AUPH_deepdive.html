Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Aurinia Pharmaceuticals is a biopharmaceutical company focused on developing and commercializing therapies for autoimmune diseases. Their primary product, LUPKYNIS (voclosporin), is the first FDA-approved oral treatment for active lupus nephritis (LN). The company generates revenue through direct sales of LUPKYNIS in the U.S. and sales to its collaboration partner, Otsuka Pharmaceutical, for European and Japanese markets. Aurinia is also developing aritinercept, a dual inhibitor for potential treatment of other autoimmune diseases, aiming to address significant unmet medical needs.
