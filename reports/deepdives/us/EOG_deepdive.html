Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

EOG Resources is an independent energy company engaged in the exploration, development, production, and marketing of crude oil, natural gas liquids (NGLs), and natural gas. The company primarily operates in major producing basins within the United States, with additional operations in Trinidad and select international areas. EOG focuses on achieving high returns and low costs through a strategy of efficient development, innovation in advanced technologies, and maintaining a strong balance sheet, aiming to maximize long-term shareholder value.

Key Risks
△ Volatile commodity prices for crude oil, NGLs, and natural gas can materially and adversely affect financial condition and results of operations.
△ Substantial and extended declines in commodity prices can render exploration and development projects uneconomic, leading to reserve write-downs and asset impairments.
△ The company has substantial capital requirements and may face difficulties obtaining necessary financing on satisfactory terms, especially in volatile credit markets.
△ Lower commodity prices reduce cash flows, potentially impairing the ability to fund capital expenditures, acquisitions, and dividend payments.
△ Dependence on gathering, processing, transportation, and export facilities can impact operations and profitability.
