Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Alliant Energy is a regulated investor-owned public utility holding company that provides electric and natural gas services to approximately 1.01 million electric and 435,000 natural gas customers across the Midwest, primarily through its subsidiaries IPL and WPL. The company's core business involves the generation, distribution, and transportation of electricity and natural gas to retail customers in Iowa and Wisconsin, with some wholesale electricity sales. Alliant Energy also has non-utility holdings managed under AEF, which include investments in transmission infrastructure, venture funds, a wind farm, supply chain solutions, and development-ready industrial sites.
