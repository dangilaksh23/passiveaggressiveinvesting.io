Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Wendy's is a global quick-service restaurant company specializing in hamburger sandwiches, chicken sandwiches, and other fast-food items. The company operates and franchises restaurants, deriving revenue from sales at company-owned locations and royalties, franchise fees, and advertising contributions from franchised locations. Wendy's differentiates itself through its focus on high-quality food prepared to order and aims to drive profitable growth through brand revitalization, operational excellence, and system optimization.
