Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

Riley Exploration Permian, Inc. is an independent oil and natural gas company focused on the acquisition, exploration, development, and production of oil and natural gas properties. The company primarily operates in the Permian Basin, a prolific oil-producing region in the United States. Its revenue is generated from the sale of crude oil, natural gas, and natural gas liquids. Riley Permian differentiates itself through its strategic focus on acquiring and developing assets in high-quality, low-risk areas within the Permian Basin, aiming for efficient production and attractive returns.
