Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

Ulta Beauty is a leading international specialty beauty retailer offering a comprehensive selection of cosmetics, fragrance, skincare, wellness, and haircare products, alongside salon services. It operates a unique retail concept that combines prestige, mass, and professional beauty products under one roof, catering to beauty enthusiasts seeking self-expression and investment. The company differentiates itself through a broad and curated assortment of brands, a convenient omnichannel shopping experience, a robust loyalty program, and a focus on exceptional guest experiences.
