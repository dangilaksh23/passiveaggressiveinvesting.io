Business Model · 10-K 2026-01

Netflix is a leading global entertainment service offering a wide variety of TV series, films, games, and live programming across diverse genres and languages. It generates revenue primarily through monthly membership fees for its streaming service, with a strategy focused on global growth and improving member experience through compelling content and a range of pricing plans, including an ad-supported option. The company aims to be the preferred choice for consumers' leisure time by continuously enhancing its technology and content offerings.
