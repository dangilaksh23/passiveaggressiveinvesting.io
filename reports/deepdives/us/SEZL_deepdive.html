Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Sezzle operates a digital shopping and payments platform designed to offer consumers, particularly Gen Z and Millennials, a flexible alternative to traditional credit. The company's core product, 'pay-in-four,' allows consumers to purchase items and pay in installments over six weeks, with options for other installment plans and a pay-in-full choice. Sezzle aims to empower younger generations financially by providing accessible, responsible credit solutions and a digital ecosystem that benefits consumers, merchants, and other stakeholders.

Key Risks
△ Reliance on a single originating bank partner (WebBank) for a substantial majority of its credit products.
△ Potential for increased fees and/or interest on its pay-in-four product, impacting consumer adoption.
△ Inability to effectively manage credit risk and potential for increased delinquencies and bad debt.
△ Competition from other buy-now-pay-later providers and traditional payment methods.
△ Changes in consumer spending habits and economic conditions impacting demand for installment payment options.
Key Initiatives
→ Enhancing the Sezzle Platform with features like Payment Streaks, price comparison tools, auto-couponing, express checkout, and customer wishlists.
→ Expanding the Sezzle Earn tab to offer more opportunities for users to accumulate Sezzle Spend through various activities and partner offers.
→ Developing and piloting new installment options like 'pay-in-five' to cater to different consumer needs and merchant preferences.
→ Strengthening its omnichannel offering through solutions like the Sezzle Virtual Card for in-store and online use at non-integrated merchants.
