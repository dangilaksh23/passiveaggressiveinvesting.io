02The Financial Picture

SEZL's financial performance in FY2025 has been nothing short of impressive. I see revenue soaring to $450.3M, representing a remarkable 66.1% year-over-year growth and a 53.0% CAGR, demonstrating strong market adoption and expansion in its digital credit services. This rapid growth is highly attractive.

What truly stands out to me is the company's exceptional profitability and cash generation. SEZL boasts a phenomenal 46.0% Free Cash Flow (FCF) Margin, placing it in the top 90th percentile of its sector, with an improving FCF trend. This indicates a highly efficient business model that converts a significant portion of its revenue into deployable cash. Operating margins are also robust at 36.1% (78th percentile), reflecting excellent operational leverage. The Return on Equity (ROE) is an outstanding 78.4% (97th percentile), also showing an improving trend, which speaks volumes about management's ability to generate value from shareholder capital.
