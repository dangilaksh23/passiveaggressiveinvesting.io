Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

Rigel Pharmaceuticals is a biotechnology company focused on developing and commercializing novel therapies for hematologic disorders and cancer. The company's strategy involves targeting critical signaling pathways involved in disease mechanisms. Rigel has three FDA-approved products: TAVALISSE for chronic immune thrombocytopenia, REZLIDHIA for acute myeloid leukemia, and GAVRETO for non-small cell lung cancer and thyroid cancer. Their competitive advantage lies in their pipeline of targeted therapies and strategic collaborations with leading research institutions and pharmaceutical partners.
