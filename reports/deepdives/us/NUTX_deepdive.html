Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

Nutex Health operates a dual-model healthcare business, generating revenue from both hospital facilities and a population health management division. The hospital division focuses on micro-hospitals, specialty hospitals, and outpatient departments offering 24/7 care, often operating as an out-of-network provider. The population health management division builds and manages provider networks, such as Independent Physician Associations (IPAs), and engages in risk-bearing contracts for patient populations. This integrated approach aims to provide accessible, concierge-level healthcare while managing patient populations through physician networks.
