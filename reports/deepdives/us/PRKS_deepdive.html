Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

SeaWorld Entertainment operates a portfolio of 13 theme parks across the United States and in the United Arab Emirates, offering a blend of animal encounters, rides, and entertainment. The company generates revenue primarily through ticket sales for park admission and in-park purchases of food, merchandise, and other items. Its competitive distinction lies in its recognized brands, extensive zoological collection and expertise, and differentiated park experiences designed to inspire guests to protect animals and the environment.
