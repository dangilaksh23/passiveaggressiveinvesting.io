Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Williams is an energy infrastructure company focused on safely and reliably delivering natural gas products to fuel the clean energy economy. The company operates a vast network of pipelines, natural gas processing facilities, and NGL fractionation facilities across the United States. Williams generates revenue by providing gathering, processing, transmission, storage, and marketing services for natural gas and NGLs to a diverse customer base, including utilities, power generators, and industrial users. Its competitive distinction lies in its extensive infrastructure, long-term customer contracts, and commitment to reliability in a growing energy market.
