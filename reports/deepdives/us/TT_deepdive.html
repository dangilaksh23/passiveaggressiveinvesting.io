Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Trane Technologies is a global climate innovator providing sustainable and efficient solutions for buildings, homes, and transportation. The company designs, manufactures, sells, and services products and systems for Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning (HVAC), transport refrigeration, and custom refrigeration. Its competitive advantage lies in its extensive global install base, a focus on recurring revenue through services and rentals, and a commitment to sustainability, aiming to reduce customer greenhouse gas emissions.
