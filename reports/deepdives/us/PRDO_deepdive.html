Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Perdoceo Education Corporation operates accredited academic institutions, including Colorado Technical University (CTU), American InterContinental University System (AIUS), and University of St. Augustine for Health Sciences (USAHS). These institutions offer online, campus-based, and hybrid degree programs from associate to doctoral levels, focusing on career-oriented and industry-relevant education. The company leverages technology, including AI and personalized learning platforms, to enhance student experiences and academic outcomes, primarily serving non-traditional adult learners seeking career advancement.
