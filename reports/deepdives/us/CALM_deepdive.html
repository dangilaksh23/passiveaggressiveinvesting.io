Business Model · 10-K 2025-07

This company is a producer and distributor of shell eggs and egg products. It generates revenue primarily through the sale of these products to consumers and businesses. Its competitive distinctiveness lies in its established production and distribution network within the egg industry, though it faces significant risks related to feed costs, avian diseases, and consumer demand fluctuations.

Key Risks
△ Volatile feed costs, primarily corn and soybean meal, significantly impact profitability.
△ Outbreaks of avian diseases like HPAI can lead to flock depopulation and reduced supply.
△ Consumer demand for shell eggs can decline due to health concerns, negative publicity, or shifts away from high-protein diets.
△ Product contamination risks can lead to costly recalls and damage consumer confidence.
△ Increases in other input costs such as packaging and delivery expenses can adversely affect profitability.
