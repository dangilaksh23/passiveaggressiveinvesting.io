Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Natural Resource Partners LP is a publicly traded partnership that owns and manages a diverse portfolio of mineral and energy assets. The company generates revenue primarily through royalty payments from lessees who extract coal, trona, and soda ash from its properties. Its competitive distinctiveness lies in its extensive land holdings and the long-term leases it has established with various resource producers.
