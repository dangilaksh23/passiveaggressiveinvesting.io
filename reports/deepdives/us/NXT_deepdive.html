Business Model · 10-K 2026-05

Nextpower is a leading global provider of integrated solar and energy technology solutions, primarily for utility-scale power plants. They are the market leader in solar tracking systems, which enable solar panels to follow the sun, increasing energy production and improving the cost-effectiveness of solar power. The company offers a platform that integrates structural, electrical, and digital solutions, including AI-driven yield management, to enhance the efficiency, reliability, and return on investment for solar projects. Their solutions are designed to support the global energy transition by making solar power more competitive and scalable.
