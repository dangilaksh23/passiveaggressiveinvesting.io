Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Altria Group, Inc. is a leading U.S. tobacco company focused on transitioning adult smokers to a smoke-free future and exploring new growth opportunities beyond nicotine and the United States. The company generates revenue primarily through the sale of cigarettes, machine-made large cigars, moist smokeless tobacco products, and e-vapor products. Altria competes through strong brand recognition, particularly with its Marlboro cigarette brand, and aims to differentiate itself by offering a diverse portfolio of nicotine products and investing in innovation.

Key Risks
△ Inability to anticipate and respond to changes in adult nicotine consumer purchase behavior and preferences, including due to economic conditions, could adversely affect the business.
△ Significant competition from existing and innovative nicotine products, including e-vapor and oral nicotine pouches, could negatively impact market share and profitability.
△ Evolving adult nicotine consumer preferences, including movement across product categories and the purchase of illicit products, have negatively impacted cigarette volumes.
△ The proliferation of illicit flavored disposable e-vapor products has hindered the growth of FDA-authorized e-vapor products.
△ The willingness of consumers to purchase premium brands is affected by macroeconomic conditions, and inability to offset volume declines with price increases could be detrimental.
