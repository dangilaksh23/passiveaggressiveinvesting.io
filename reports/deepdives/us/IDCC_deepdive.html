Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

InterDigital is a global research and development company that designs and licenses foundational wireless, video, and AI technologies. They generate revenue by licensing their extensive patent portfolio to companies that incorporate these innovations into connected products and services, such as smartphones, consumer electronics, and cloud-based services. Their competitive distinction lies in their significant patent portfolio, deep expertise in wireless and video standards, and their role as a leading pure R&D and licensing entity.
