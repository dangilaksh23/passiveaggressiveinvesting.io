Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Hecla Mining Company, established in 1891, is a producer of precious and base metals, primarily silver, gold, lead, and zinc. The company extracts these metals from its mines in North America, processes them into concentrates or doré, and sells them to smelters, traders, and refiners. Hecla's competitive distinction lies in its long operating history, its portfolio of established mines in North America, and its focus on managing operations safely, environmentally responsibly, and cost-effectively.
