Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Waste Connections is the third largest provider of non-hazardous solid waste management services in North America, operating across 46 states in the U.S. and six Canadian provinces. The company generates revenue by collecting, transferring, and disposing of solid waste, with additional services including recycling, renewable fuels generation, and specialized treatment for oil and gas exploration waste. Waste Connections differentiates itself by targeting secondary and rural markets, often securing exclusive contracts and optimizing asset positioning with strategically located disposal sites to achieve operational efficiencies and profitability.
