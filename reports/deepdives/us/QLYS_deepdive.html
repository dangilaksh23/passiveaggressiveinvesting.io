Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Qualys provides a cloud-based platform offering integrated IT, security, and compliance solutions. The company helps organizations identify and manage IT assets, discover and prioritize vulnerabilities, quantify cyber risk, and implement remediation actions. Its platform addresses the increasing complexity of IT environments by offering a unified view of security and compliance posture across on-premises, cloud, and endpoint systems, delivered through a software-as-a-service model.
