Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

Abeona Therapeutics is a commercial-stage biopharmaceutical company focused on developing and commercializing cell and gene therapies for life-threatening diseases. Their lead product, ZEVASKYN, is the first and only FDA-approved autologous cell-based gene therapy for treating wounds in patients with recessive dystrophic epidermolysis bullosa (RDEB), a severe genetic skin disease. The company also has a pipeline of AAV-based gene therapies for ophthalmic diseases and leverages its internal manufacturing capabilities to support its development and commercialization efforts.
