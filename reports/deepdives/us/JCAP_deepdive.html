Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

Jefferson Capital, Inc. is a debt recovery solutions provider that purchases portfolios of consumer receivables, such as credit card, automotive, and utility debts, at a discount. The company then manages these receivables by working with individuals to help them repay their obligations and achieve financial recovery. They also offer debt servicing and portfolio management for credit originators and earn revenue through credit card acquisition programs. Their competitive advantage lies in their expertise in evaluating and managing charged-off accounts, proprietary valuation methods, and strong relationships with large financial service providers.

Key Risks
△ Deterioration in economic or inflationary environments can reduce consumers' ability to pay debts and the value of purchased receivables.
△ Deterioration in global credit market conditions could reduce the volume of nonperforming loans available for purchase.
△ The company may not be able to continually replace nonperforming loan portfolios with sufficient additional portfolios at appropriate prices.
△ The company may not be able to collect sufficient amounts on purchased nonperforming loans to fund operations.
△ Increased personal bankruptcy and insolvency filings could negatively impact collections on unsecured receivables.
Key Initiatives
→ Continue to purchase portfolios of nonperforming loans through spot sales and forward flow agreements.
→ Leverage expertise in evaluating and managing charged-off accounts to manage higher risk portfolios.
→ Maintain strong relationships with large financial service providers.
→ Evaluate opportunities to purchase portfolios with a mix of performing and nonperforming accounts.
