Business Model · 10-K 2025-08

H&R Block provides tax preparation services, financial products, and small business solutions, blending digital tools with human expertise. The company generates revenue by preparing tax returns for individuals and small businesses, offering related financial products like prepaid cards and loans, and providing bookkeeping and payroll services. H&R Block differentiates itself through its established brand, extensive network of tax professionals, and a combination of in-person and digital service offerings.
