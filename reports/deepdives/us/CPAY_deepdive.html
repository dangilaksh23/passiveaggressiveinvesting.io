Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Corpay is a global corporate payments company that provides businesses with a suite of solutions to manage and pay expenses, including accounts payable automation, cross-border payments, commercial card programs, vehicle payment solutions, and lodging payment solutions. The company aims to help businesses save time and money by digitizing payments, offering customized controls, and providing robust reporting and analytics. Corpay differentiates itself through its specialized technology platforms, proprietary payment acceptance networks, and recurring, volume-driven revenue models.
