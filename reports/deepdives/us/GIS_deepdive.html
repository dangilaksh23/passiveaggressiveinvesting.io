Business Model · 10-K 2025-06

General Mills is a global manufacturer and marketer of branded consumer foods, offering over 100 brands across six continents. The company generates revenue by selling a diverse portfolio of human and pet food products, including snacks, cereals, convenient meals, pet food, and dairy items. Its competitive advantage lies in its strong brand recognition, extensive distribution network, and ability to innovate and meet evolving consumer preferences for taste, nutrition, and convenience.

△ Fluctuations in the cost of raw materials and packaging due to external conditions like weather, scarcity, and commodity market volatility.
