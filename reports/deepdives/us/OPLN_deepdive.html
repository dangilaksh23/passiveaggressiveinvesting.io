Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

OPENLANE operates a leading digital marketplace for wholesale used vehicles, connecting automotive manufacturers, dealers, rental companies, and fleet operators as buyers and sellers across the United States, Canada, and Europe. The company facilitates millions of vehicle transactions annually, generating revenue through transaction fees and the sale of value-added services like financing, logistics, and technology solutions. OPENLANE differentiates itself through its integrated technology platform, extensive data analytics, and a comprehensive suite of remarketing services, aiming to simplify wholesale transactions for its customers.
