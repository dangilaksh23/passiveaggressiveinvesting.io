Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

Paysign is a vertically integrated provider of prepaid card products and processing services, offering solutions for corporate, consumer, and government entities. They generate revenue from all stages of the prepaid card lifecycle, including fees from cardholder activity, program management, and settlement. Additionally, Paysign provides life science technology solutions for blood and plasma collection organizations, earning revenue from licensing, hosting, and consulting. Their competitive advantage lies in their powerful, high-availability payment platform with advanced fintech capabilities that can be seamlessly integrated with client systems, offering flexibility, scalability, and cost savings.
