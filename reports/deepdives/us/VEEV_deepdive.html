Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

Veeva Systems provides industry-specific cloud software, data, and consulting solutions to the global life sciences industry. Their offerings help pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and medical device companies streamline operations from research and development through commercialization, aiming to bring products to market faster and more efficiently. Veeva's competitive advantage stems from its deep industry focus, enabling rapid adaptation to regulatory changes and development of targeted solutions on its proprietary Veeva Vault platform.
