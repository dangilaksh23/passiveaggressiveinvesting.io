Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

CubeSmart is a self-administered real estate company that owns, operates, develops, manages, and acquires self-storage properties primarily in the United States. The company generates revenue by leasing storage units to residential and commercial customers on a month-to-month basis, offering both standard and climate-controlled units. CubeSmart differentiates itself through its focus on providing high standards of physical attributes and service, and by strategically acquiring and developing properties in targeted markets with strong demographic growth and demand for storage.
