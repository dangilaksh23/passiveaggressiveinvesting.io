Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Service Corporation International is North America's largest provider of deathcare products and services, operating a vast network of funeral homes and cemeteries across 44 states, eight Canadian provinces, the District of Columbia, and Puerto Rico. The company generates revenue by selling cemetery property, funeral services, and related merchandise, both at the time of need and on a preneed basis. Its competitive distinction lies in its unparalleled geographic scale, established Dignity Memorial brand, and the operational efficiencies gained from its combination funeral home and cemetery locations.
