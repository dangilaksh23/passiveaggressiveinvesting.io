Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

TransMedics is a medical technology company that has developed the Organ Care System (OCS), a portable, warm perfusion technology platform designed to preserve donor organs for transplantation. Unlike traditional cold storage, the OCS keeps organs alive and functioning outside the body, enabling optimization and assessment, which aims to increase transplant volumes and improve patient outcomes. The company also offers the National Organ Procurement (NOP) service, a comprehensive solution for organ procurement, OCS perfusion management, and transplant logistics, including aviation and ground transportation, to streamline the process for transplant centers.
