Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

Ross Stores operates two off-price retail chains, Ross Dress for Less and dd's DISCOUNTS, offering branded apparel, accessories, footwear, and home fashions at significant discounts to department and specialty store prices. The company targets value-driven customers, primarily from middle to lower-middle income households, by leveraging opportunistic purchasing strategies to acquire in-season, brand-name merchandise. Its competitive advantage lies in its ability to offer a wide assortment of recognizable brands at compelling prices within an organized, treasure-hunt shopping environment.
