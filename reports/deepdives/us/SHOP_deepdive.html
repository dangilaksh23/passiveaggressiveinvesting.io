Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Shopify provides an all-in-one e-commerce platform that empowers businesses to start, run, and grow by enabling sales across online storefronts, physical retail, and various other channels. The company offers tools for merchants to manage inventory, transactions, marketing, and customer relationships, aiming to provide cutting-edge technology for success in a competitive market. Shopify's business model is driven by merchant success, with users increasingly adopting additional solutions and upgrading plans over time.
