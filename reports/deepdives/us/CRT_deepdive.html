Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

CRT is a trust that generates revenue from net profits interests in oil and gas properties primarily located in Texas, Oklahoma, and New Mexico. The company's revenue is derived from a 90% net profits interest in royalty and overriding royalty properties, and a 75% net profits interest in working interest properties. These interests are carved from producing and nonproducing oil and gas assets owned by XTO Energy or other ExxonMobil affiliates. CRT's competitive distinction lies in its ownership of these net profits interests, which provide a stream of income based on the profitability of underlying oil and gas production, rather than direct ownership of the assets.

Key Risks
△ The actual reserves recovered and the timing of production may differ substantially from current estimates.
△ Net profits income from working interest properties is reduced by production and development costs, affecting profitability.
△ Excess costs on certain conveyances must be recovered before net profits income is generated.
△ Changes in oil and natural gas prices can impact future development plans on the underlying properties.
△ The Trustee is prohibited from selling net profits interests without significant unit holder approval or if Trust gross revenue falls below $1 million for two consecutive years.
