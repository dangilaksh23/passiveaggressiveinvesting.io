Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

CSX Corporation is a leading transportation company in the United States, primarily providing rail-based freight transportation services. It operates an extensive rail network east of the Mississippi River, connecting major population centers, ports, and industrial facilities. The company generates revenue by transporting a diverse range of goods, including merchandise, intermodal containers, coal, and through its trucking subsidiary. CSX differentiates itself through its scheduled service plan, aiming for improved customer service, asset optimization, and cost reduction, which it states leads to strong free cash flow generation.
