Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

ADT Inc. is a leading provider of security, interactive, and smart home solutions for residential and small business customers in the U.S. The company generates revenue primarily through its approximately 6.1 million security monitoring service subscribers, offering a range of alarm systems, smart cameras, and automation devices. ADT differentiates itself through its strong brand reputation, built over a long history, and its strategic evolution towards a platform-centric model focused on integrated home intelligence, exemplified by its proprietary ADT+ application.
