Business Model · 10-K 2025-07

Cintas Corporation provides essential products and services to over one million businesses across the U.S., Canada, and Latin America, helping them maintain a professional image and ensure clean, safe facilities. The company's core offerings include uniform rental and servicing, facility services like restroom supplies and mats, and first aid and safety products. Cintas differentiates itself through a comprehensive service model delivered via extensive local delivery routes, aiming to be a one-stop shop for businesses preparing for their workday.
