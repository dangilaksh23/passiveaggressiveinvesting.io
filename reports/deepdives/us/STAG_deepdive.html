Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

STAG Industrial, Inc. is a Real Estate Investment Trust (REIT) focused on acquiring, developing, and operating industrial properties across the United States. The company generates revenue primarily through rental income from its portfolio of industrial buildings. STAG differentiates itself by focusing on a granular, quantitative approach to acquiring individual industrial properties in Tier 1 markets, aiming for reliable cash flow and long-term asset appreciation.
