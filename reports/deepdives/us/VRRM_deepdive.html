Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Verra Mobility is a leading provider of smart mobility technology solutions focused on making transportation safer, smarter, and more connected. The company generates revenue by offering integrated, data-driven solutions across three segments: Commercial Services (toll and violations management, title and registration), Government Solutions (automated safety and traffic enforcement), and Parking Solutions (commercial parking management). Verra Mobility differentiates itself by bringing together vehicles, hardware, software, data, and people to solve complex transportation challenges for a diverse customer base, including large commercial fleets and government agencies.
