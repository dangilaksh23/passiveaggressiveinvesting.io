Business Model · 10-K 2025-08

KLA Corporation is a leading supplier of advanced equipment and services that drive innovation across the electronics industry, particularly in semiconductor manufacturing. The company provides sophisticated process control and enabling solutions for producing wafers, integrated circuits (ICs), and printed circuit boards (PCBs). KLA's offerings help manufacturers improve product yields, reduce waste, and lower costs, thereby enhancing their profitability and return on investment. With a focus on enabling technological advancements and high-volume production, KLA plays a critical role in the development and manufacturing of next-generation electronic devices.
