Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Magnite operates as the world's largest independent omni-channel sell-side advertising platform, specializing in automating the purchase and sale of digital advertising inventory. The company provides technology solutions for publishers to manage and monetize their inventory across various channels, including Connected TV (CTV), and for buyers (advertisers, agencies, DSPs) to access this inventory. Magnite differentiates itself by offering a transparent, independent marketplace that facilitates intelligent decision-making and automated transactions at scale, serving as a crucial alternative to "walled garden" ecosystems.
