Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

The Trade Desk is a global leader in advertising technology, providing a platform for ad buyers to create, manage, and optimize digital advertising campaigns across various formats, channels, and devices. Leveraging artificial intelligence and a rich ecosystem of partners, the company enables superior reach and decisioning for its clients, which include advertising agencies and advertisers. Revenue is generated through platform fees based on client spend and value-added services, positioning it as a key player in the rapidly growing programmatic advertising market.
