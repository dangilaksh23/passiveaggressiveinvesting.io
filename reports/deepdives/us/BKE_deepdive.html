Business Model · 10-K 2026-04

The Buckle is a US-based retailer specializing in medium to better-priced casual apparel, footwear, and accessories for fashion-conscious men, women, and kids. The company operates 440 retail stores across 42 states, focusing on a wide selection of denims, tops, and other casual wear, complemented by personalized customer service and value-added services like free hemming. Its competitive distinction lies in its unique store environment, a broad assortment of both brand-name and private-label merchandise, and a strong emphasis on customer loyalty programs and personalized styling.
