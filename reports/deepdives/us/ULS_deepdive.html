Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

UL Solutions is a global leader in safety science, providing Testing, Inspection, and Certification (TIC) services, along with related software and advisory offerings. The company helps over 80,000 customers in more than 110 countries ensure their products meet safety, security, and sustainability standards, facilitating global market access. Its competitive distinction lies in its long heritage dating back to 1894, extensive technical accreditations, and the iconic UL Mark, which appears on billions of products worldwide.
