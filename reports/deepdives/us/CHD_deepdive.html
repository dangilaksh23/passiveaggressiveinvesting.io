Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Church Dwight Co., Inc. is a consumer goods company that manufactures and markets a portfolio of household, personal care, and specialty products. The company generates revenue primarily through the sale of its diverse range of branded products, which include well-known names in categories like baking soda, laundry detergent, and oral care. Its competitive distinction lies in its established brands, broad distribution network, and a history of strategic acquisitions that expand its product offerings and market reach.
