Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Hilton is a global hospitality company that operates and franchises a portfolio of hotels across luxury, lifestyle, full-service, focused-service, and all-suites categories. The company generates revenue through management and franchise fees from third-party hotel owners, licensing fees, and sales at its consolidated hotels. Hilton's competitive advantage lies in its extensive brand portfolio, its award-winning Hilton Honors loyalty program with 243 million members, and its long history of innovation in the hospitality industry.
