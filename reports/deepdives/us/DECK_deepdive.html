Business Model · 10-K 2026-05

DECK designs, markets, and distributes innovative footwear, apparel, and accessories under its proprietary brands HOKA, UGG, and Teva. The company targets both everyday casual lifestyle use and high-performance activities, differentiating itself through fashion, performance, authenticity, functionality, quality, and comfort. DECK generates revenue by selling its products through a wholesale channel to retailers and distributors, as well as directly to consumers via its e-commerce websites and company-owned retail stores.
