Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Grand Canyon Education (GCE) is an education services company that partners with colleges and universities across the United States to provide comprehensive technological, infrastructural, and operational support. GCE leverages significant investments in proprietary technology, including its Halo learning management system and AI tools, to manage academic functions, faculty recruitment, admissions, and financial aid for its partners. The company aims to scale these services to support hundreds of thousands of students, distinguishing itself through its integrated technology solutions and operational expertise in the higher education sector.
