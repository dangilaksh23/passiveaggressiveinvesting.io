Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

Buda Juice is pioneering the UltraFresh™ juice category by offering cold-crafted citrus-based beverages that are never heat-treated or HPP-processed. They leverage an end-to-end cold chain platform to deliver consistently fresh, safe, and nutrient-rich juices, lemonades, and wellness shots to grocery retailers. Their competitive distinction lies in solving the industry's challenge of delivering true freshness at scale, eliminating the need for retailers to invest in specialized infrastructure or operations.
