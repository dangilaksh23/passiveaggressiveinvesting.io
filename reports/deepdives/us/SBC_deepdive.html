Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

This company provides management services to medical clinics, including franchised and independently operated locations, primarily in Japan. It generates revenue through compensation for these management services. The company's competitive distinctiveness is tied to its brand image and its ability to ensure compliance with various laws and regulations by its business partners and clinics, which is crucial for maintaining revenue streams and reputation.
