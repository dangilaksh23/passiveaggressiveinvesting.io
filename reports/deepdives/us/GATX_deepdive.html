Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

GATX Corporation is a global leader in leasing transportation assets, primarily railcars and aircraft spare engines. The company owns and manages extensive fleets across North America, Europe, and India, serving diverse industries such as transportation, chemical, petroleum, and food/agriculture. GATX generates revenue by leasing these assets to customers, with a competitive advantage stemming from its large, diversified fleet and long-standing industry presence. The company also engages in aircraft spare engine leasing through its wholly owned subsidiary and joint ventures.
