Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

MPLX LP is a diversified master limited partnership that owns and operates midstream energy infrastructure and logistics assets, alongside providing fuels distribution services. The company generates revenue by gathering, transporting, storing, and distributing crude oil, refined products, natural gas, and natural gas liquids (NGLs). Its competitive distinction lies in its extensive network of pipelines, terminals, storage caverns, and processing facilities strategically positioned across the United States, enabling it to offer comprehensive wellhead-to-market services.
