Business Model · 10-K 2025-09

Nutanix offers a unified hybrid multicloud computing platform designed to simplify the deployment and operation of distributed applications and AI. The company's software platform enables organizations to run and manage their infrastructure across core data centers, edge locations, and public clouds, providing a consistent operating model. Nutanix aims to increase customer agility and accelerate innovation by supporting a wide range of workloads, from business-critical applications to AI and cloud-native services, while offering flexibility in hardware and cloud choices.
