Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Colgate-Palmolive is a global consumer goods company focused on health and well-being for people, pets, and the planet. It operates in two main segments: Oral, Personal, and Home Care, and Pet Nutrition. The company leverages its strong brand portfolio, including Colgate and Palmolive, to drive organic sales growth and deliver shareholder returns through innovation, operational efficiencies, and data-driven capabilities. Its products are marketed in over 200 countries and territories worldwide.
