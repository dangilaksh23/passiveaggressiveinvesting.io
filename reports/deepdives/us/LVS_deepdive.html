Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Las Vegas Sands Corp. is a global developer and operator of large-scale "Integrated Resorts" that combine premium accommodations, world-class gaming, entertainment, retail, and convention facilities. The company generates revenue primarily from its gaming operations, focusing on the profitable mass market segment, alongside significant contributions from hotel stays, food and beverage, retail leasing, and convention services. Its competitive distinction lies in its geographically diverse, best-in-class properties in Macao and Singapore, offering a wide array of amenities to attract both leisure and business travelers, and its strong brand recognition.
