Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Exzeo Group, Inc. provides technology and operations solutions for property and casualty (P&C) insurance carriers and their agents. Leveraging its proprietary Exzeo Platform, the company offers a suite of software and data analytics applications to streamline core insurance functions like underwriting, policy administration, and claims management. Exzeo generates revenue through variable fees based on premium managed or transaction volume, enabling clients to scale operations efficiently without significant upfront technology investment. Its competitive distinction lies in its integrated, configurable platform designed to enhance workflow efficiency and support data-driven decision-making in the insurance industry.
