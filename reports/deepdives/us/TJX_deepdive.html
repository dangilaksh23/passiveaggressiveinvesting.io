Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

TJX is the leading off-price retailer of apparel and home fashions globally, operating over 5,200 stores and six e-commerce sites. The company offers a constantly changing selection of quality, branded merchandise at prices significantly below traditional retailers, creating a 'treasure hunt' shopping experience. Its competitive advantage stems from an opportunistic buying strategy, flexible business model, and ability to leverage global vendor relationships and buying power across its diverse retail chains.
