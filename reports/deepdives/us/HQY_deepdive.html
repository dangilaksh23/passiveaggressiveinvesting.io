Business Model · 10-K 2026-03

HealthEquity provides technology-enabled services that help consumers manage their healthcare savings, spending, and investment decisions. The company administers tax-advantaged health savings accounts (HSAs) and other consumer-directed benefits (CDBs) like FSAs and HRAs, offering payment processing, personalized benefit information, a healthcare marketplace, and investment advice. HealthEquity aims to capitalize on the trend of increased consumer responsibility for healthcare costs by offering a comprehensive platform for managing healthcare finances.
