Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Catalyst Pharmaceuticals is a biopharmaceutical company focused on developing and commercializing treatments for rare neurological and neuromuscular diseases. Their primary product, AGAMREE, is an anti-inflammatory drug approved for Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy (DMD) in patients aged 2 and older, aiming to provide efficacy with potentially fewer side effects than traditional corticosteroids. The company also markets FYCOMPA for epilepsy. Catalyst generates revenue through product sales and has a strategic focus on protecting its intellectual property and expanding access to its therapies.
