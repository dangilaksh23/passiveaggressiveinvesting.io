Business Model · 10-K 2025-12

Broadcom is a global technology leader providing a broad range of semiconductor and infrastructure software solutions. The company designs, develops, and supplies complex digital and mixed-signal devices, as well as software for managing IT infrastructures. Its products are used in diverse applications including AI data centers, networking, wireless devices, servers, storage, and broadband access. Broadcom differentiates itself through high-performance design, integration capabilities, and a focus on category-leading solutions, leveraging both internal R&D and strategic acquisitions.
