Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Wingstop is the world's largest fast-casual chicken wing restaurant chain, operating primarily as a franchisor with over 3,050 locations globally. The company generates revenue through royalties, advertising fees, and franchise fees from its independent franchisees, who own approximately 98% of the restaurants. Wingstop differentiates itself through its focus on cooked-to-order wings, tenders, and chicken sandwiches, offered in 12 bold flavors, complemented by seasoned fries and fresh vegetables, aiming to become a Top 10 Global Restaurant Brand.
