Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Etsy operates two-sided online marketplaces connecting creative entrepreneurs with buyers globally, focusing on unique and handmade goods. The company generates revenue through transaction fees, payment processing fees, listing fees, and optional seller services like on-site advertising. Etsy differentiates itself through its focus on human connection, creativity, and sustainability, aiming to foster entrepreneurship and economic impact.
