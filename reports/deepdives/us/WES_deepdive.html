Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Western Midstream Partners, LP is a limited partnership that provides midstream infrastructure and services primarily for the oil and gas industry. The company generates revenue by transporting, processing, and storing crude oil, natural gas, and natural gas liquids. Its competitive distinction lies in its extensive network of pipelines and processing facilities, strategically located in key North American basins, enabling it to offer integrated solutions to its customers.
