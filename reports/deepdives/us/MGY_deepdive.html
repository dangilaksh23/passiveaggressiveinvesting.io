Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Magnolia Oil Gas Corporation is an independent oil and natural gas company focused on acquiring, developing, exploring, and producing oil, natural gas, and natural gas liquids. The company's operations are concentrated in South Texas, targeting the Eagle Ford Shale and Austin Chalk formations. Magnolia aims to deliver shareholder value through steady, moderate production growth, disciplined capital spending, and generating free cash flow for returns to shareholders.
