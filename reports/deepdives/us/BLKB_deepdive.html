Blackbaud is the leading provider of AI-powered cloud software and services for social impact organizations, including nonprofits, educational institutions, and companies with corporate social responsibility initiatives. The company helps these organizations with fundraising, engagement, education, financial management, and grantmaking. Blackbaud leverages its extensive philanthropic data and connected workflows to enable these entities to operate more efficiently and effectively achieve their missions, distinguishing itself through deep sector expertise and intelligent, integrated solutions.
