Business Model · 10-K 2026-02

Garrett is a technology leader specializing in emission reduction and energy efficiency solutions for the mobility and industrial sectors. The company designs, manufactures, and sells highly engineered turbocharging, air and fluid compression, and electric motor technologies. Its products are crucial for improving fuel economy, energy efficiency, and meeting emissions targets in both internal combustion engine (ICE) and zero-emission vehicles, making it a key partner for original equipment manufacturers and aftermarket distributors.

Key Risks
△ Failure to innovate and adapt to evolving automotive industry needs, particularly the shift to electric vehicles, could harm the business.
△ Intense competition from established players, integrated OEMs, and well-funded startups poses a threat to market position.
△ Reliance on major customers, with the top ten accounting for 62% of net sales, creates significant risk if relationships change or customers face financial difficulties.
△ Economic downturns and industry-specific challenges in the cyclical automotive sector can adversely affect sales and operations.
△ The success of new, pre-development stage electrification technologies is not guaranteed and requires significant investment.
