Risks & Invalidation
  • Government Budget Cuts / JJM Delays 100% government customer exposure means any reduction in JJM/AMRUT/NTPC allocations directly impacts revenue. Union Budget 2026-27 is the key watch event. JJM Phase 2 funding reduction of >20% would require thesis reassessment.
  • NTPC BESS Cancellation / Delay NTPC Battery Energy Storage System contract is a key growth driver. Any cancellation or multi-quarter delay removes one of three revenue pillars and invalidates the 45% growth thesis.
  • Promoter Open-Market Selling Promoters are currently buying at ₹138–148. Any open-market selling above ₹200 would signal a change in insider conviction. Hard exit trigger.
  • Government Customer % Below 80% If commercial customers rise to >20% of revenue mix, the sovereign-grade revenue quality degrades and C1 score drops materially.
  • Two Consecutive PAT Declines At 14x P/E the market is pricing in growth continuation. Two quarters of PAT decline (even on revenue growth) would signal margin compression and trigger re-rating risk.
  • IPO Lock-Up Expiry IPO was November 2024. Anchor investor lock-ups typically run 90 days. Watch for institutional selling pressure in Feb-May 2025 period. Now absorbed — reduced risk.
  • Working Capital Cycle Stretch Debtor days currently <60 (excellent for govt EPC). If this stretches above 90 days, it signals payment delays from government customers and cash flow risk.
