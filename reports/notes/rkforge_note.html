C4 = 0 — Stage 4 with net loss Q2 FY26 (₹9.5 Cr). My framework treats Stage 4 as a hard block on new positions regardless of other scores. Watching only until Q4 FY26 results and a technical reversal.
  • Forgings manufacturer — automotive (passenger vehicles, commercial vehicles), railways, and aerospace. Reported a net loss in Q2 FY26 of ₹9.5 Cr. The company is in a cyclical trough at the time of this note.
  • Moderate regulatory exposure, same as the rest of the auto metal forming sector. PLI-AUTO incentivises, but the OEM is the customer. The railways and aerospace exposure doesn't change the C1 rating materially at this revenue mix.
  • The promoters issued warrants at ₹588 against a CMP of ₹471 at scoring — they are buying future shares at a 25% premium to market. That is a significant internal conviction signal and is why C2 scores reasonably well despite the loss quarter.
  • C3 scores maximum — not because the business is cheap on current earnings (it's loss-making), but because the mid-cycle recovery case implies meaningful upside from the depressed price. My research estimate of ₹900+ from ₹471 is a recovery thesis, not a going-concern valuation.
  • Q4 FY26 PAT turning positive, and the 50-DMA beginning to slope upward. Both conditions together would signal that the cycle is turning and the chart is responding. Until both, I stay on the watchlist.
